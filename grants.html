Have you ever wanted to make something really awesome?

Never fear! DIB Lab Grants are here!

DIB Lab Grants are a way for everyone to make their wildest dreams a reality. Whether you want to make a 3D printer from scratch, a robot that solves Rubix cubes, or a floating kinetic art piece you can apply for a grant so that you have the funding and support to make it happen!

Apply for a DIB Lab Grant using the template below as your guide. When you have it all filled out, send it to Ms. Jimenez to find out what the next steps are in the process.

View Grant Application Template

Once you've opened the application template, go to File > Make a Copy to create a copy in your Google drive that you can edit. If you are in the Lower School and don't have a Google account but would like to apply for a grant please see Ms. Jimenez.
